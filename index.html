U-Bus is a bus service that runs to your schedule. The U-Bus app displays all available routes and stops. You hail U-Bus from the app and it gives you a pick up time from your nearest stop. U-Bus provides walking directions to your nearest stop, to get you there on time. And if that wasn't cool enough U-Bus uses smart-route technology to pick up the most people for each trip minimizing the cost normally associated with this kind of flexibility.

U-Bus is not just about the individual though. By using existing infrastructure in a highly optimised way we reduce traffic and carbon emissions. U-Bus is about increasing the enjoyability of public transport while decreasing the amount of waste and pollution involved in traditional travel options.

No more waiting for jammed buses with fixed schedules. You get a small roomy bus with a guaranteed seat, wifi, air-conditioning, at the time you need it. No more overpriced taxis just to get the flexibility you need. U-Bus can even be booked up to 24 hours in advance and will give you your stop and pick up time when you book. Or select a specific stop for a trip later in the day and U-Bus will be there for you - even giving a reminder message when it is time to head for your stop based on your location and average walking time.

FAQ

How much will a trip on U-Bus cost?
It will differ from city to city. But rest assured it will be a fraction of the cost of a taxi service and only slightly more than the regular bus service.
What type of buses will you use?
We intend to use minibuses seating roughly 12 people in comfort.
What routes/stops do you cover?
When U-Bus comes to your city we will start with already busy existing bus routes. Then based on our customer hailing patterns we will add new stops and routing. So our routes and stops will organically update to match our customers needs.
