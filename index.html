U-Bus is not your normal old bus service. You hail U-Bus from the U-Bus app and it gives you a pick up time from your nearest stop. U-Bus then uses smart-route technology to pick up the most people for each trip minimizing the cost normally associated with this kind of flexibility. But theres more to U-Bus then just a flexible service...

U-Bus was born out of the realisation that we can't just have smarter greener cars for everyone, this would still make traffic worse and not even make a real dent on CO2 emissions. We can't wait for well intentioned infrastructure plans involving trains, undergrounds, and trams (no matter how well run the future version is). U-Bus will use existing infrastructure in a highly optimised way - roads, bus lanes, bus stops - leveraging todays technology - smartphones, GPS, and where viable electric/hybrid vehicles. U-Bus will deliver the service of the future today. Let's get on a schedule of change that can make a real difference. Increasing quality of service, value for money, and decreasing CO2.

The more you use U-Bus the better it gets. When you open the U-Bus app it displays all available routes and stops. When U-Bus comes to your city we will start with already busy existing bus routes. When you hail a bus you get a pick up time for your nearest bus stop. Then based on our customer hailing patterns we will add new stops and routing. So our routes and stops will organically update to match our customers needs.

No more waiting for jammed buses with fixed schedules. You get a small roomy bus with a guaranteed seat, wifi, air-conditioning, at the time you need it. No more overpriced taxis just to get the flexibility you need. U-Bus can even be booked up to 24 hours in advance and will give you your stop and pick up time when you book.

FAQ

How much will a trip on U-Bus cost?
It will differ from city to city. But rest assured it will be a fraction of the cost of a taxi service and only slightly more than the regular bus service.
What type of buses will you use?
We intend to use minibuses seating roughly 12 people in comfort.
Why should I fund the development of U-Bus?
The old ways are failing. Taxis are expensive and heavy polluters. Bus services are cheap, low quality, and poorly optimised. U-Bus is a chance to make a difference today. To show what we can do to improve service and make a positive environmental difference. The old way will only get us more of the same. The new way can be here today. Maybe U-Bus can stem the tide of new car owners who feel their only option is to own a car - they can't afford taxis everyday and bus times aren't flexible enough.
How will funds be used?
The funds raised in this bitstarter campaign will fund the initial development and testing of the U-Bus app.
What will happen once the app is ready to be deployed?
Initially it is our intention to partner with a carefully chosen commercial bus service who will provide the fleet and drivers for our first routes. U-Bus will do what we do best through provision of technology and vision.
